Who We Are

RN MASH INTERNATIONAL

RN MASH INTERNATIONAL is a dedicated spare parts supplier specializing in Bajaj Auto three-wheeler vehicles and tuk-tuk components. Based in Aligarh, we supply premium-quality auto spare parts to customers worldwide.

With decades of experience, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, consistent quality, and exceptional customer service. All our products meet OEM standards and are trusted by clients globally.

We have successfully completed 690 export shipments to 4 verified international buyers, reflecting our growing global presence and commitment to excellence.
